My First Blog Entry

Is this what power feels like?

My pages load! It all begins with as simple text:This is my first cool website. Only a few hours into my ~internet art~ course endowes a new technique of creation. Creation! Creation at my figure tips. I AM GOD here. I may not be infinite, but this is now my space. Its nice to mark a spot. I couldn't ask to be well traversed, but I do have an ownership now atleast. I always felt so restricted by the internet before coding. So restrained to mainstream site like google and social media platform. It so impersonal, I can put my name places, make memberships, pretend, rent my spot. But now I am a spot. People can now join me and follow me, become a member to the cult that is me. But is this website about world domination, maybe eventually but that's besides the point. I have a space on the internet. A blank infinite page, space I can put anything on. I exist now in the digital space. This is my voice. Can you hear me yet. Hello. Anyone out there listening to my words?
